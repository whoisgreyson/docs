## Staff Badges

In an effort to provide transparency and precision with service delivery, all Agents that hold a permanent position on the team have a recognizable staff badge on the `/whois` command, viewable by everyone. This badge is not obtainable by request or by purchase, **please do not ask for it** to be added to your account.

Our temporary team members (on trial, contract, and so on) may not have a visible badge on the user lookup command. Do not be alarmed, however: staff that do not hold this badge may still hold a career with us\! Check our support server—official staff have a role above everyone else.

## Verified Badge

To maintain authenticity and promote known users across the industry, notable accounts within our network of users have the opportunity to have the Verified Badge applied. This badge serves as a significance of their dedication, notable status, and authenticity. It is not available for purchase or request.

| Aspect      | Description                                                                                  |
| ----------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Selection   | Collection's management team reviews the user's significance and importance to the community |
| Application | The leadership team applies the badge and notifies the user of their achievement.            |

This badge is a privilege, not a right. We reserve the right to revoke this badge as we see fit, for any reason.

<Warning>
  This badge is not available for purchase or request. Doing so will not receive a reply.
</Warning>
