<Card title="What is the aim of the game?" icon="square-4" iconType="solid" color="ff5757">
  The possibilities are endless. For inmates, you can choose to roleplay as a civil prisoner, abide by the daily schedules, and make friends with others. Alternatively, you can choose the chaotic lifestyle—recruit allies, cause chaos, craft restricted weapons, and attempt to escape.&#x20;



  For prison guards, your job is to rid the prison of chaos and ensure all prisoners are following orders and the schedule.
</Card>

<Card title="Does the game have paid features?" icon="square-5" iconType="solid" color="ff5757">
