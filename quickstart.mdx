### 3. Where do I report a rule violation?

All rule violations are handled by our moderation team. To submit a report against a user, please open a ticket through our Discord server. **We cannot take reports through the DMs of any moderator.**

### 4. Can I contact the owner?

If you open a ticket in our communications server, our team will forward the message if it is of importance to the owner.

### 5. How can I ask a question that isn't listed here?

Ask on our Discord! You can try asking the question in a public channel or opening a ticket to obtain an answer from a moderator.
