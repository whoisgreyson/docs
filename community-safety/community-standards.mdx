---
title: "Community Standards"
description: "How we govern our community safely."
---

Our community evolves on a regular basis; and it's important that we evolve to assure safety remains paramount. Our community standards dictate the behaviours we strictly prohibit within our community. Disregarding these standards could lead to user bans, suspensions, warnings, or other moderations as required.

* Online dating.

* Requesting the personal information of others or propagating personal details without consent.

  * You cannot ask for any personal information besides age/gender.

* Distributing exploits, viruses, or other malicious content.

* Luring or encouraging illegal activity/self-harm.

* Violating Discord or Roblox's Terms of Service.

We operate on a zero-tolerance policy with rule violations. Users who are found to be acting in contravention to these standards may be subjected to moderation action being taken by our moderation team.

<Info>
  If you feel unsafe, let us know. We'll take action based on the report and any findings we discover.
</Info>
