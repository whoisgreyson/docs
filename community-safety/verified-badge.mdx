---
title: "Verified Users"
description: "Our exclusive badge for authentic Discord accounts."
---

As a token of our appreciation, recognized or well-known members of our community may be eligible for our **Verified User** badge. This is recognized on your user profile when you run the `/whois` command in our Discord server.

| Procedure   | Description                                                                                                                                                        |
| ----------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Selection   | All users who are selected for the application of this badge undergo a background and investigative process to ensure they are an important user to our community. |
| Application | Our leadership team takes action and applies the badge upon approval by the Community Director and Manager.                                                        |

The selection and application processes take time. Please do not pester our team or ask if you can have the badge. If the badge is applied to your account, you will be notified and it will be recognizable on your user profile!

<Info>
  To maintain the authenticity and integrity of the badge, we do not sell the badge or take requests for its application. We will let you know if you're selected as a badge holder.
</Info>
