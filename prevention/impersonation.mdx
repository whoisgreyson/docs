Collection respects the quality and integrity of all users that partake in the server and its services. We do not, under any circumstances, tolerate any form of impersonation or fraudulent misidentification. Impersonating a member of our team is prohibited and will lead to action being taken by our team, up to and including a blacklist being put into effect.

## Offence Criteria

We take impersonation seriously. Identifying as a member of our team when you're not may lead to action being taken by our team. This action could include a warning flag to other users, a system blacklist incurred, or a removal from our community as a whole. To be considered impersonation, a user must first:

- Identify with our logo, name, and descriptive factors that represent our official service, or;
- Inform other users that you are the official service when you are not, or;
- Performing phishing, scams, or other fraudulent activity while utilising our service media.

All of this behaviour is prohibited and will lead to consequences. In the event where our assets are utilised for impersonation, a DMCA will request will be filed in conjunction with corrective action.
