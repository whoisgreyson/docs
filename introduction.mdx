Welcome to Rockwood Correctional Facility, a prison roleplay experience with the player's enjoyment in priority. Whether you choose the life of an inmate and try to escape the prison, or pursue a prison guard career and protect the facility from riots and disasters—the choice is yours. Since being founded in 2025 by Greyson and Nick, the mission to provide an enjoyable prison roleplay experience continues to be paramount.

### Documentation

This documentation has been created with the intention of providing a transparent approach to our systems and game information. While we keep this information as updated as we can, we make no guarantees that it will always be the most accurate source of information. **Contacting our moderation team is always the best option.** We are here to help!
