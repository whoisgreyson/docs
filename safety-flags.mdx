---
title: "User Safety Flags"
description: "Our version of community protection."
---

The moderation team may occasionally apply safety flags to suspicious or dangerous accounts on Discord. They are viewable by running the `/whois` command using our custom bot in the Discord server. These flags serve a dual purpose in our community—to maintain a record internally and to warn our community of accounts we officially deem to be suspicious. We currently have the following flags:

* <Icon icon="circle-exclamation" iconType="regular" color="ff0000" />

  &#x20;This user is known to spread misinformation.

* <Icon icon="triangle-exclamation" iconType="regular" color="ff0000" />

  &#x20;This user is not a Rockwood moderator.

* <Icon icon="octagon-exclamation" color="ff0000" />

  &#x20;This user is involved with malicious activities.

Accounts who possess any safety flag on the `/whois` command should be considered dangerous and be warned before offering them any sort of permission or authority. **We are not responsible for damages caused as a result of disregarding the safety flags we impose.**

<Info>
  Safety flags are applied upon receiving a valid report of dangerous behaviour, or actions that are observed directly by our team. Users may contact our moderation team through the Discord to inquire about the flag or request its removal.
</Info>
