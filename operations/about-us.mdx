Our team does the tedious work for you, so all you need to do is sit back and relax. Our accumulation of Agents take the time to review each report and take it seriously. Since 2025, we've made it our mission to detect and flag accounts that have violated our policies, which in turn protects and secures communities across the platform.

### How does the security function work?

Simply add our Discord bot to the server you would like to protect, and give it the permissions it asks for. Once the bot has been added, you're done\! Collection will passively screen members as they enter your server and ensure they do not have any active records. If they do, server staff will be notified and the account will be actioned accordingly.

### How do I know if a report is real/legitimate?

Collection takes pride in offering an unbiased, equal approach to each report. By default, all reports are double-checked and verified by our team to ensure false positives are limited. We require evidence for all reports and retain this evidence internally for future reference. If you wish to obtain the evidence for an associated case or receive more information, contact us.
