## Report Intentions

Before filing a report, please ensure you have sufficient evidence to support your claim. Filing a report is a serious action to take, but is necessary in most circumstances. Please ensure you provide your full statement, applicable evidence, and any other associated information that Agents should know.

<Warning>
  Witch-hunting or filing reports against users out of anger or frustration is not tolerated. Please do not file a report simply because you aren't friends with them anymore\!
</Warning>

## Steps to Filing a Report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Gather Background Information" titleSize="p">
    Sourcing and saving the details of the situation is vital for your report. Leaving gaps in your story or failing to obtain the full story will lead to a denial of your report.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a Ticket">
    Create a ticket in our support server. Be sure you attach the evidence, your statement, and additional information that will assist our team in processing your report quickly and reasonably.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Internal Investigation">
    After your report has been received, our team will assign an Agent to your case to conduct a thorough investigation. We do not publish the specific steps to this investigation, however, we utilise a comprehensive process to mitigate the risk of false positives and biased reports.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Decision of Verdict">
    The Agent associated with your case will decide a verdict for your case. If the report is deemed valid, staff will move forward and create a blacklist for the reported account(s). If the report is invalid, your report ticket will be closed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The processes involved in the report system will take time to complete. We ask that you exercise patience with our team as we work to resolve the reports as fast as we possibly can. Extra time is taken to thoroughly investigate the situation in full and clarify gaps, if any, in the timeline.

Rushing our team or pestering them with @mentions, Direct messages, or similar behaviour will not lead to a heightened response time. Rather, it will lead to delays as our team responds to your message rather than conducting appropriate investigations.
