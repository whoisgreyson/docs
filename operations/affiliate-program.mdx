Our team is always on the hunt for new servers to affiliate with. Whether you have 50 members or 50,000—an affiliation with Collection enhances your server safety and encourages collaborative events.

## Steps to Apply

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a Ticket">
    Please open a support ticket in our Discord. This is the easiest and fastest way to obtain a response from our team and to initiate the affiliation process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide your Details">
    After opening the ticket, please provide your server name, member count, owner, domain (industry/topic), and your reason for the affiliation. After receiving all of this information, our team will forward it to our communications lead for processing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Our Team will Review your Request">
    Our communications lead will review the information you have submitted and verify their accordance with our affiliation standards. If we believe your server is a match for an affiliation, we'll let you know.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  We do not release our affiliation standards for confidentiality and equality purposes. Please do not request them.
</Warning>
