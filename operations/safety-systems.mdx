## Bot Features

Our Discord bot is equipped with the necessary systems to rid your server of malicious users. Upon joining your server for the first time, server management can run the `/server scan` command to run a thorough check of all users in the server. The bot will return any active records that correspond to accounts found in the server. The bot **will not action** these accounts, you will simply be notified.

When users join your server, the bot will automatically run a passive check for their history with Collection. If their history returns a match for a blacklist, the bot will take action as configured by the server's owner.

## Support Structure

Our team is able to assist you as we're available. To contact us, please reach out directly through our support server on Discord. From there, navigate to the support channel and open a ticket. Please explain your inquiry with precision to reduce response times and improve quality care and service delivery.

We kindly request that you refrain from contacting our team by any means other than through our support server. We will not respond to any communication related to Collection that is done outside of the server boundaries.

## Retrieving Evidence

To safely store evidence, we encourage you to upload it to a popular CDN website, like Imgur or Google Drive. Our team does not download files or click links to domains that are untrustworthy, including shortened links.

**Video evidence is the best evidence. **While we do accept screenshots and images, we do prefer videos and media in the form of an MP4. This alleviates some of the risk of fabricating evidence and is harder to manipulate.
