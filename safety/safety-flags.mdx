When you join our Discord server, our custom bot manages several aspects of the community's operation—including user safety. We receive numerous reports on a regular basis, and it's hard to keep track of all the dangerous users using a database system. That's why we've created **safety flags**.

### What is a Safety Flag?

A safety flag is applied to a user's Discord account when we can confirm they are involved in activities justifying the flag. It displays a warning on the `/whois` command using our custom bot based on the flag we've added. The flags that are currently active include the following:

> <Icon icon="circle-exclamation" color="ff0000" /> This user is known to spread misinformation

> <Icon icon="triangle-exclamation" color="ff0000" /> This user is not a Rockwood moderator

> <Icon icon="octagon-exclamation" color="ff0000" /> This user is involved with malicious activities

These flags are added to accounts to warn other users of their previous history, as well as to maintain an internal record when conducting investigations and background checks.

<Info>
  **Note:** Users may contact our support system to inquire about a safety flag or to request its removal from an account.
</Info>
